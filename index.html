alexandra rivera

about

alexandra rivera (alex) is a new york-based graphic designer and video editor. born and raised in queens, new york, alex is currently pursuing an associate's degree in digital media at laguardia community college. aside from being a student, she is also a president’s society ambassador, perkins peer mentor, and student success mentor at laguardia. in her art, alex takes inspiration from the early 2000’s and loves incorporating her femininity in her work. after graduating from laguardia, she plans to transfer to queens college, where she will hone her graphic designing skills.
